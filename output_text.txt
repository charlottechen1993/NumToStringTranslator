Pierre Vinken , sixty one years old , will join the board as a nonexecutive director Nov. twenty ninth . 
Cipher Data Products Inc. posted a net loss of fourteen point two million dollars , or ninety seven cents a share , for its fiscal first quarter , compared with net income of three point eight million dollars , or twenty seven cents a share , a year ago . 
Revenue for the quarter ended September thirtieth fell twenty percent , to forty one point three million dollars from fifty one point nine million dollars in the year-earlier period . 
The debentures , due in two thousand fourteen , have a coupon of seven and three quarter percent , payable semiannually .
